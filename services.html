services

groomers

At Radiant Rodents, we offer a gentle and thorough grooming service designed especially for small pets like hamsters, guinea pigs, chinchillas, and other furry friends. Our team provides careful brushing, nail trimming, and coat checks to keep your pet healthy, clean, and comfortable. We use safe, rodent‑friendly products and take extra care to ensure every animal feels calm and relaxed throughout the process. Your little companion will leave looking fresh, tidy, and truly radiant!


medical care

At Radiant Rodents, we provide compassionate basic health support to keep your small pets feeling their best. Our team offers wellness checkups, routine health assessments, and gentle monitoring to help spot common issues early. We work closely with trusted local veterinarians for anything requiring professional medical treatment, ensuring every hamster, guinea pig, chinchilla, or mouse receives safe, appropriate care. With a calm environment and a focus on your pet’s comfort, we’re here to help your little companion stay happy, healthy, and radiant.


donate

At Radiant Rodents, we believe every little creature deserves comfort and care while waiting for a loving new home. That’s why we welcome donations from kind‑hearted supporters who want to help us provide the best food, enrichment, bedding, and health checks for our rescued rodents. Every contribution—no matter the size—goes directly toward creating a safe, happy, and nurturing environment for the animals in our care. With your support, we can ensure each furry friend has the brightest possible start before finding their forever family.
